Our Mission

Our intention is to offer simple, mindful tools that create meaningful shifts in how we move, breathe, and live — through gentle, accessible yoga and heart-centered guidance.

  • We support reconnection: calm in the everyday, strength in softness, and balance on and off the mat.
  • We meet you where you are, honoring your body, your breath, and your journey.
  • Every breath, every pose, and every moment of awareness is an invitation to return home to peace.

Tara offers sessions designed to meet every body where they’re at — gentle, mindful, and accessible, focused on releasing tension, restoring flow, and cultivating awareness.

Paired with the vibroacoustic bed experience — soothing sound vibrations that calm the nervous system and ease the body into deep rest — sessions create space where healing unfolds naturally.

Meet Tara

Tara Copp is the founder of Tarasana Yoga and a certified yoga instructor specializing in gentle, restorative, and accessible practices.

After spending over thirty years in the service industry, Tara knows firsthand how hard constant movement and long hours can be on the body and mind. Her own journey through pain and fatigue led her to yoga — a practice that taught her how to slow down, listen inward, and rebuild balance from the inside out.

Now, through her teachings with Tarasana at CareMore Health & Wellness, she shares what she’s learned: caring for your body is not a luxury, but an essential act of growth. Her classes offer a calm space to breathe, soften, and reconnect — one mindful moment at a time.

“I believe in the quiet power of simple practices — a slow breath, a gentle stretch, a steady vibration. These are the moments that help us return home to peace.” — Tara Copp

Vibroacoustic Therapy

Touchless meditative massage

What it is

Vibroacoustic Therapy (VAT) combines low-frequency sound vibrations with therapeutic music. You recline on a vibroacoustic bed with embedded tactile transducers while using headphones, creating a calming, whole-body experience.

Benefits & support areas

  • Stress & anxiety support
  • Pain & discomfort support
  • Muscle tension & spasticity support
  • Sleep disturbances support
  • Mood & emotional well-being
  • Mental clarity & focus

What to expect

  • Arrive in comfortable, loose-fitting clothing
  • Recline on the vibroacoustic bed (headphones + optional eye mask)
  • Frequencies are selected based on your needs
  • Relax and let sound + vibration do the work
  • Sessions last 15–60 minutes, based on your goals
15 minutes

Quick reset: stress relief, power nap, or a first-time introduction.

30 minutes

A full experience for relaxation and steady wellness maintenance.

45 minutes

Deeper meditation states and comprehensive stress reduction.

60 minutes

Deepest session: multiple frequencies for chronic concerns and profound relaxation.

Therapeutic frequencies

VAT uses ultra-low frequencies (about 20–120 Hz) to send gentle vibrations through the body’s water-rich tissues, encouraging relaxation, tension release, and a return to balance.

30–40 Hz

Commonly associated with attention, memory, and mental clarity (40 Hz is often highlighted for brain health support).

40–60 Hz

Often linked with energizing effects, improved alertness, and enhanced mood.

60–80 Hz

Connected to balance, harmony, and deeper relaxation.

80–120 Hz

Associated with gentle stimulation, circulation support, and muscle relaxation.

Note: These descriptions are wellness-oriented and not a substitute for medical care. If you have a medical condition, ask your provider what’s appropriate.
