Who We Are

We are Tarasana — a collective of practitioners in different fields of wellness creating an accessible path to health and happiness.

Meet Tara

Tara is a certified yoga instructor, life coach (soon-ish), and certified bad booty bitch. Tara’s goal is to help guide you toward better health, greater mobility, decreased pain, inner peace, and self love.

What We Do

We eat the elephant one bite at a time. No magic bullet. No easy answer. No quick fix.

  • Stand side by side with you to face life’s big challenges.
  • Break them down into manageable goals.
  • Work toward them with a multimodal approach — mobility, nutrition, meditation, breathwork, and more.

It’s not about rushing. It’s about building a better today that leads to a brighter tomorrow.

Why We Do It

Growing up teaches us that our bodies and minds aren’t invincible — and the way we care for them matters.

  • We witness how time and stress change the people we love.
  • We learn the real cost of ignoring pain, mobility, and mental wellbeing.
  • We realize small daily choices shape our future.
  • We choose to turn that lesson into service — helping others live better.

These revelations shaped not only our personal paths, but our life’s work — focusing our energy, talents, and experience on helping you live your life fully.
